LANS Property Management

Thank you for visiting LANS Property Management. We work with students, working adults & families seeking rental housing throughout Central Minnesota, including St. Cloud, Sauk Rapids, Foley, Clear Lake & Melrose Minnesota. We offer homeowner association management and work with your Board of Directors to ensure your property values are maintained and strengthened. We provide the services you would expect from a small and dedicated to excellence company.

We work with you to find the perfect fit with a variety of 1,2,3 or 4 bedroom apartments. We make applying for your new home easy with our on-line application process and offer the convenience of applying in our office as well if you need assistance. Once you are our resident we want to keep you! We pride ourselves on having great resident relations. Our office staff, as well as our on-site caretakers and maintenance team, are here to assist when needed. We help you understand your lease, deal with any maintenance concerns or building problems and keep our common areas neat and clean. We want our apartments to feel like your home!

For property owners in St. Cloud and throughout Central Minnesota, we assist you with professional management services that include reliable oversight of the daily tasks involved in property ownership. We value the trust that property owners place in us and will assure you that we are always looking out for the best interest of your investment. We take care of properties as if they were our own. We provide assistance with pricing, listing and marketing, screening prospective residents and showing your properties. We inspect properties and take care of all your maintenance concerns. We welcome the opportunity to work with you.
